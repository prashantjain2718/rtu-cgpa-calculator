How SGPA is Calculated (RTU Formula)

SGPA is calculated using: SGPA = Σ (Credit × Grade Point) / Σ Credits

How CGPA is Calculated

CGPA is based on cumulative performance: CGPA = (Previous CGPA × Total Previous Credits + Current SGPA × Current Credits) / Total Credits
